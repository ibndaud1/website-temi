My Story

Growing Up

I was brought to Lagos Island exactly two (2) months nine (9) days after I was born, precisely to Ita Faaji on the Island to live with my maternal grand parents.My maternal grandma who was a food vendor ,who specifically sells amala with varieties of Yoruba soup to go along.

I remember vividly, that one of her customers who hailed from Ilorin in Kwara state had a song for me those days that goes thus : omo dudu kororo lo ndan , omo dudu kororo lo ndan , omu miliki otutu lawo omo dudu kororo lo ndan and I danced seriously to this song as it is nothing but a eulogy of my radiant skin.
